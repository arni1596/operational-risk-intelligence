# Operational Risk & Decision Intelligence System

A deterministic decision-support system that translates routine operational activity into early risk signals and decision-ready summaries, with full traceability and documented judgment.

## Why this exists
I built this system to surface where operations fail quietly, before they fail publicly.

## Problem this system addresses
Operational teams generate large volumes of activity data, yet leadership decisions are often made without clear visibility into emerging risk, backlog health, or process breakdowns.

This system prioritizes clarity, accountability, and early intervention over dashboards, prediction, or automation for its own sake.

## Design principles
- Clarity over complexity
- Explainability over automation
- Deterministic logic over black-box models
- Human judgment remains mandatory

## Current status
Phase 1: batch analysis, explicit risk logic, and static decision briefs designed to support review and escalation.
